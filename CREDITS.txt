Vortex's Universal Achievement System (VUAS)
Copyright (c) 2026 Vortex

VUAS references architectural patterns from Blade of Agony (dual-handler persistence, SystemTime relay, cheat detection, icon dimming) and Stupid Achievements (OptionMenu subclass, notification queue, replacement-aware matching) but all implementations are original except BoA's Base64+ROT encode/decode functions which were directly adapted.
